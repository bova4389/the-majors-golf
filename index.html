Basic Bros — Majors Pick'em

The unofficial Major tournament golf pool for the Basic Bros crew.

How to Play

  1. Pay your $25 entry fee. Multiple entries allowed — $25 each, no maximum.
  2. Submit your picks before the tournament lock time (first tee time, Day 1).
  3. Select one golfer from each of the 6 tiers. Choose a backup per tier in case your pick WDs before play.
  4. Your score = sum of your best 4 of 6 golfers' scores (to par). Lowest total wins.
  5. Daily high score (single round, best 4 golfers) pays $25/day × 4 days.
  6. $1 per entry per tournament funds the Season Bonus Pool — best average finish across all 4 majors wins it.

Scoring Notes

  • Best 4 of 6: Your 4 lowest golfer scores count each day. All 6 remain active throughout.
  • CUT penalty: (Day 1 score + Day 2 score) × 2, applied to Days 3 & 4.
  • WD mid-tournament: Worst combined D1+D2 in the full field × 2.
  • Late WD (pre-tournament): Backup pick activates automatically — no penalty.
  • Tiebreaker: Best 5 of 6, then best 6 of 6.

Payout Structure

  • $25 per entry × number of entries = total pot.
  • $100 reserved for daily high score payouts ($25 × 4 days).
  • $1 per entry goes to the Season Bonus Pool.
  • Remaining pot split (top 5): ~50% · 25% · 13% · 7% · 5%.
  • Season Bonus Pool: paid out after The Open Championship concludes.
